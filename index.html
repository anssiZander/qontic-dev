Q-Ontic Lab

Interactive quantum simulations and notebooks for comparing alternative representations of quantum mechanics.

Comparative Apps Pilot-Wave Apps Notebooks Research Team

Q-Ontic Lab develops browser-based tools for exploring how different quantum representations describe the same physical phenomena. Apps are designed for immediate exploration, while notebooks combine explanation, context, and embedded simulations.

Comparative Apps

Standalone simulations that compare multiple quantum representations or interpretations side by side.

Pilot-Wave Apps

Standalone trajectory-focused simulations for exploring pilot-wave dynamics, guidance laws, and wave-packet motion.

Interactive Notebooks

Longer explanatory pages that combine narrative, context, and embedded interactive demonstrations.
